Nephrology Department

The Nephrology Department at Panimalar Medical Hospital specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of kidney-related disorders. Our team of experienced nephrologists provides comprehensive care for conditions such as chronic kidney disease, acute kidney injury, hypertension, and kidney transplants. We are committed to improving kidney health and overall well-being through advanced treatments and personalized care.
