Dermatology Department

The Dermatology Department at Panimalar Medical Hospital specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of skin, hair, and nail conditions. Our team of experienced dermatologists offers advanced care for a wide range of dermatological issues, including acne, eczema, psoriasis, skin infections, and cosmetic dermatology. We are committed to providing personalized care to help you achieve healthy and radiant skin.
